Called when a child view is attached along with embedding information.

This method will be called at most once after the child is added.

The implementation should invoke the callback once the event has been handled.

ViewContainerListener::OnChildUnavailable ( uint32  child_key)

Called when a child view has become unavailable.

A child may become unavailable for many reasons such being unregistered by its application, abnormal termination of its application, or cycles being introduced in the view tree.

To complete removal of an unavailable child, this view component must call RemoveChild() on its view with |child_key|.

The implementation should invoke the callback once the event has been handled.
